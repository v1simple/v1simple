## On AI-assisted development

Portions of this project were developed with AI-assisted pair programming.

The architectural decisions, the Valentine-philosophy framing from the
historical project instructions, the module boundaries, and every choice about
what belongs in firmware vs. a phone app are the maintainer's. AI was used the
way most engineers use it
today — as a pair-programmer for refactors, test generation, and prose
cleanup — not as an author of record.
